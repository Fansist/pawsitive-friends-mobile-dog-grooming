Good To Know

Frequently asked questions

A few honest answers before you call or text.

How does mobile dog grooming actually work?
The grooming van comes directly to your home and parks outside, usually in your driveway or on the street. Your dog is groomed in the fully equipped van, so there's no drop-off, no car ride for your dog, and no waiting room.
Do you groom all dog sizes and breeds?
Yes, full-service grooming is offered for dogs of all sizes and coat types, including baths and nail trims.
What areas of San Diego do you serve?
Pawsitive Friends is a mobile service covering greater San Diego. Call or text 619-395-9440 to confirm the van can reach your specific neighborhood.
How do I book an appointment?
Text-to-book is available, so you can skip the phone tag, just send a text to 619-395-9440. You're also welcome to call, and service is available in English or Spanish.
What days are you open?
Open Monday through Saturday. Call or text to check available appointment times for your day.
